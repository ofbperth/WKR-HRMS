# User Guide

## Reporter

1. Login with email/password or Google if allowed.
2. Accept the PDPA notice after login.
3. Open `Report Incident`.
4. Fill event time, unit, risk code, title, description, and severity.
5. Patient HN is optional.
6. Track submitted incidents in `My Reports`.

## UnitManager

1. Open `Unit Dashboard` for unit summary.
2. Use `Unit Incidents` to review unit cases.
3. Open `Unit RCA` to complete RCA when requested.
4. Use `Unit Actions` to follow owner progress and overdue actions.

## RMTeam

1. Use `RM Dashboard` for daily operational overview.
2. Open `Triage` for new incidents.
3. Update severity/status/risk classification.
4. Review `RCA Review` and approve or request revision.
5. Track `Actions` and verify completed work.
6. Generate monthly reports in `Reports`.

## Executive

1. Open `Executive Dashboard` for summary cards and charts.
2. Use `9 Safety Goals` for high-level risk trend.
3. Open `Monthly Report` for printable summaries.
4. Executive views avoid sensitive incident details by default.

## Admin

1. Use `Admin Console` as the central entry.
2. Manage users, roles, units, and risk codes.
3. Configure Google login policy in `Auth Settings`.
4. Review `Audit Logs`.
5. Run protected jobs in `Admin Automation`.
